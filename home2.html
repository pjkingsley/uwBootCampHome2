Hello and thank you for your interest. I'm a Washington native living in Seattle. At the University of Washington I studied history, philosophy, law, literature, and drama. So naturally I became a server at upscale restaurants. I spent 10 years in the restaurant industry as a bussboy, server, bartender, wine stewart and general manager. After ten years, though, I decided to return to school to learn more about wine and enrolled at the Northwest Wine Achadamy, part of South Seattle College. Course work there included: atomic chemistry, vineyard management, science of food and wine pairing, enology, wines of the world (I & II), sensory and production. I then started my career as an Enologist and was hired as an assistant winemaker. This was well and good for three years but then I realized I was living in Seattle, by which I mean I was broke. To return to financial stability I started working retail wine sales which could provide a steady paycheck, if not a lucrative one. Which is why I am now here. Please look over my work and judge for yourself I can be of help to you. Thanks again! Peter
